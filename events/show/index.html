Show & Sale

April 2, 2022 from 10am to 4pm

The Puget Sound Gesneriad Society and the Seattle African Violet Society are pleased to announce our annual joint event to be held at the Conservatory on April 2, 2022 from 10am to 4pm.

On view in the Seasonal Display House will be a display of show-quality gesneriads including African Violets, Streptocarpus, Sinningias, Columneas, Aeschynanthus, Episcias, and Kohlerias. These and many other unusual and hard to find plants will be for sale in the propagation greenhouse to the rear of the main house.

Here is your chance to acquire a new type of house plant or add to your own gesneriad collection. Knowledgeable folks will be on hand to answer questions and give culture tips.
